The Strategy Follows Your Goals

Every company is unique. Our AI implementation strategy therefore always starts with your specific challenges and goals. We focus on the areas that have the greatest positive impact.

Typical Starting Points:

  • Unclear processes: We visualize and optimize workflows before supporting them with AI.
  • Communication gaps: AI can help improve information flows and close "black holes".
  • Knowledge silos: Building a central, AI-supported knowledge base to make expert knowledge accessible to everyone.
  • Long onboarding/offboarding times: Standardization and partial automation for efficiency gains.
  • Tool overload: Strategic selection and integration of AI tools that truly fit the need, instead of blind activism.
